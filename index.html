Book Summary Corner

Books I’ve read & what they taught me

The Alchemist

Author Paulo Coelho

A shepherd boy follows his dream across the desert to find treasure. But the real treasure? Self-discovery and trusting the universe.

Atomic Habits

Author James Clear

This book teaches how tiny changes can lead to massive results. Make habits obvious, attractive, easy, and satisfying. Boom.

The Fault in Our Stars

Author John Green

A heartfelt love story between two teens with cancer. Beautiful, funny, and sadness filled with tears. It’s not about how long you live, but how well.
